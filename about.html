Who We Are

Chipons ng Bayan! is more than just a snack brand; it is a celebration of Filipino flavor and community. Our journey started with a simple goal: to create a snack that everyone in the country can enjoy and be proud of. We believe that the best snacks come from the best ingredients, which is why we look to the sea for inspiration. Our brand represents the hardworking spirit of the people and the rich resources of our local waters.

The Shrimptastic Quality

What makes our products special? It is the Shrimptastic Crunch! Shrimp is the star component of every single serving we produce. We use natural shrimp flavor to ensure that every bite feels like a trip to the ocean. We take fresh Philippine shrimp, season it perfectly, and transform it into the crispy chips you love. We make sure that our chips are never dull�they are light, airy, and packed with a savory punch that keeps you reaching for the bag.

Something for Everyone

At Chipons ng Bayan!, we know that everyone has a different favorite taste. That is why we developed nine delicious varieties: Classic, Barbeque, Adobo, Spicy Sweet, Salted Egg, Buttered, Cheese, Habanero Lime, and Salted Vinegar. Whether you want something simple and salty or something bold and spicy, we have a chip for you. We use simple words to describe our food because the taste speaks for itself. We are dedicated to providing high-quality snacks that bring joy to your snack time, every single day!
